Vegan Baking 101

Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

Makes 12 cookies

225 grams all-purpose flour
3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
140 grams vegan butter
200 grams cane sugar
1 tablespoon flaxseed meal
2 tablespoons water
1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
340 grams dark chocolate

Method

  1. In medium bowl, whisk flour, salt, and baking soda together. Set aside.
  2. Using a mixer with paddle attachment fitted, cream butter and sugar together on medium speed for about 3 - 4 minutes, or until light (60% aeration)
  3. Mix flaxseed meal, water, and vanilla extract together. Add mixture to mixer and mix until combined. Scrape down.
  4. Add dry ingredients all at once and mix on low speed until 90% incorporated. Scrape down.
  5. Add dark chocolate and mix until fully combined. Do not mix more than you need to. Scoop dough into 12 (2 oz/80 gram) mounds the size of golf balls. Transfer to parchment lined baking sheet and refrigerate overnight
  6. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Seperate cookies on baking sheet so they have room to grow.
  7. Bake cookies for 15- 20 minutes, rotating halfway through. Pull when edges are golden brown and middle appears slightly under-baked. Sprinkle with sea salt and let cool completely before eating.
Note:
If you do not have a stand mixer you can use a spatula or wooden spoon to mix dough.
The mixing time for creaming your butter and sugar may be a bit longer (5-6 minutes).
